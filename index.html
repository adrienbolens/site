Activities

We meet every two weeks to discuss the following topics:

  • Training Fully connected networks (FCN), convolutional neural networks (CNN) and deep neural networks (DNN) for classification and regression problems.
  • The inner works of LSTM and RNN architectures.
  • Reinforcement learning, i.e. q-Learning to simulate the dynamics of a Hamiltonian with a universal set of unitary gates.
  • Gaussian-Process Optimization for attosecond photoionization.
  • Neural ordinary differential equations.
